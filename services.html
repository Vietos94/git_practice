Our Services

Party Favors - Themed Playdough Kits

Our themed playdough kits make fantastic party favors that extend the celebration beyond the party. Tailored to fit any birthday theme, these kits come with vibrant, non-toxic playdough and themed toys and props. Whether in larger or smaller formats, they provide endless hours of creative play. As a wonderful take-home gift, they encourage engaging, screen-free play that continues the fun and supports creative development long after the party ends.

Baby Shower or Welcome Baby Gifts

Our sensory bottles are one-of-a-kind gifts, offering a unique blend of sensory stimulation and developmental benefits. Paired with other items from our collection, they create a stunning gift package that's sure to impress. Perfect for baby showers or welcoming new arrivals, our sets deliver exceptional quality and impact.

Subscription Service

Our subscription service is designed with busy parents in mind, making it easy to provide your children with enriching sensory experiences. Each month, you'll receive a new playdough kit, eliminating the need for preparation and seamlessly integrating creative play into your routine. Subscribers enjoy priority access to our seasonal kits, which are available in limited stock, ensuring you get first choice of these special themes. Plus, you benefit from exclusive pricing, making it even more convenient to keep playtime fresh and exciting.

Party Décor

At The Playground, we pour our heart into crafting beautiful themed decor for kids' birthday parties. Each celebration can be brought to life with decor that matches the party's theme, creating a memorable experience for every child. Our attention to detail ensures that every element is perfect, adding a touch of magic and joy to your child's special day.
