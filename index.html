Creating Inspiring
Sensory Play

Hi, I'm Chloé,

As a mom of 3 with a background in Early Childhood Education, I've seen how sensory play sparks and supports holistic development.

Our journey began during the pandemic, when I wanted to create a nurturing and stimulating space for my first son. Noticing a gap in HCMC, I was inspired to create The Playground — a place where fun meets learning.

Our mission? To offer safe, premium, screen-free tools that foster growth, creativity, and joy in every child's playtime.

Did you know?
Sensory play benefits everyone

Boosts Brain Power:

Enhances understanding through exploration.

Builds Communication:

Expands vocabulary as kids describe what they experience.

Sparks Imagination:

Fuels creativity with engaging activities.

Develops Motor Skills:

Improves fine motor skills for everyday tasks.

Mess-Free Tips:

Contain It: Use a tray or mat.

Keep It Simple: Choose easy-to-clean materials.

Quick Clean-Up: Use wipes and make cleaning fun!
