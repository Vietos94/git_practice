About Us

At The Playground,
we embrace the power of sensory play

As a mom of 3 with over 10 years of experience in Early Childhood Education, I've seen how engaging activities ignite creativity and nurture a child's development.

Our journey began at home, where I crafted playdough for my first son as a way to provide sensory play stimulation during a time of months-long lockdown. It quickly became more than just a fun activity — it kept him engaged for hours, sparking his imagination, fostering calm, and even serving as a therapeutic tool for his eczema.

Recognizing a need for more sensory play options in Ho Chi Minh City, I created The Playground to offer high-quality products that seamlessly blend fun and learning.

We provide premium sensory bottles, imaginative playdough kits, party decor, party favors, and thoughtful gifts for baby showers and new arrivals. Our subscription service brings fresh, enriching play experiences monthly.

Our mission is to nurture young minds with safe, high-quality play tools, providing screen-free alternatives that encourage exploration, imagination, and growth.

How it all began

Our story is a true boy-mom adventure! As a busy working mom with 2 under 2 and then 3 under 3, life can be a whirlwind of chaos and joy.

Our journey began at home, during the challenging COVID lockdown, when I was determined to create a nurturing and stimulating environment for my first son.

Building on my background in early childhood education, I knew the importance of sensory play in supporting his overall development. So, I set out to find creative, screen-free activities that would provide both stimulation and excitement.

One of my biggest concerns at the time was my son's eczema, especially on his hands, which was tough to treat due to constant use. I discovered that playdough could be a therapeutic tool and began crafting recipes to create a high-quality, skin-friendly playdough. My experiments led to our signature playdough, enriched with coconut oil, which not only improved his condition but also offered endless hours of fun, soothing, bonding playtime.

As our family grew, the benefits of sensory play naturally extended to my younger sons, nurturing their development in creative and calming ways.

Our experience with sensory bottles further deepened my understanding of sensory play's impact. For my infants and toddlers, these bottles were essential tools for fostering sensory awareness and growth. As they grew older, the bottles evolved into captivating tools for interactive activities, color mixing, and sensory science exploration, enhancing both their learning and imaginative play.

And then, combining my love for birthday party decor with meaningful play experiences, I began creating themed playdough kits. I noticed how these kits extended the fun and learning beyond the celebration, and sensory bottles became thoughtful gifts for baby showers and new arrivals, offering a calming and engaging experience for babies and older children alike.

Seeing a gap in sensory play offerings in Ho Chi Minh City, I was inspired to create solutions that seamlessly blend fun and education. At The Playground, my goal is to share these enriching experiences with other families by offering tools that nurture growth, creativity, and joy in every child's playtime.
